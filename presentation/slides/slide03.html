Why It Matters — Real-World Use Cases

The same abstract problem shows up in logistics, finance, computing, and security.

Cargo & Container Loading

Pick packages for a truck or aircraft when capacity is fixed and each item has a profit and weight.

Capital Budgeting

Choose investment projects under a fixed budget where each project has an expected return.

Cloud Resource Allocation

Schedule jobs on a server with limited memory or CPU to maximise throughput or revenue.

Cutting Stock

Choose cut patterns from raw material to maximise yield within available stock.

Cryptography

Merkle–Hellman knapsack cryptosystem encodes messages as 0/1 selections.

Game AI & Inventory

Limited-slot inventory or loadout selection — classic 0/1 with weight = slot cost.
